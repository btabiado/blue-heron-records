About

Many personalities, one house band.

The BHR House Band is a multi-talented, multi-personality family of musicians specializing in fun, engaging performances for restaurants, bars, and corporate events. The same crew shows up under a range of themed configurations — so you can book the exact vibe your event calls for, from New Orleans blues to an 80s dance party. Full bio coming soon.
