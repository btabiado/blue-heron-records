About.

Carol Jenkins is a composer, singer-songwriter, musician, and member of the band The Carol’s. Born and raised around people who value music and art, she was always immersed in some form of artistic expression.

The daughter of a pastor, Carol found the stage at a very young age in church — singing, dancing, and public speaking — and in school took part in music recitals, musicals, and performing groups. While completing her bachelor’s in Architecture and Construction Science, she began songwriting, got her first guitar, and has never looked back.

“With love felt for so many styles of music — you won’t always hear the same genre, but you’ll always feel the heart and soul.”
