The story

Where it all began

In 2024, Blue Heron Records walked into Picture-Minded Studio in Lemont, Illinois, and recorded its very first session — the moment BHR officially became a record company.

We tracked two original songs that day, with founder and producer Joe LeDuc behind it all. These photos capture the people, the room, and the energy of a label's first day on the record.
